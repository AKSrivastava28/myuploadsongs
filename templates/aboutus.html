About us

Mini Beats is a lightweight, browser-based music player built with Python Flask and Tailwind CSS. It allows users to upload, stream, and download their favorite songs all in one place.

This project was created to explore full-stack web development and bring together music lovers who want a simple way to manage and play their songs through the browser.

Whether you're a developer, a music producer, or just someone who enjoys good tunes, Mini Beats is here to give you a clean, minimal, and efficient audio experience.

Built with ❤️ using Flask, Python, and Tailwind CSS.
